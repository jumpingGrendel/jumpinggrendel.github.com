How to Play

Find a word that contains the 3 letters shown on the license plate, in that order.

Example: C R T → CARROT

You have 3 tries to guess correctly.

Note: Finding a correct word that is not the Longest or Shortest does not use up a try!

A new puzzle is available every day at Midnight UTC.
