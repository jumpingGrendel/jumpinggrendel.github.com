How to Play

Find a word that contains the 3 letters shown on the license plate, in that order.

Example: C R T → CARROT

Goal: Find the Shortest or Longest word to WIN!

Scoring:

  • Longest Word: 100 points (WIN!)
  • Shortest Word: 85 points (WIN!)
  • Valid Word: +5 points
  • Wrong Guess: -10 points

Game Over: 3 wrong guesses.

A new puzzle is available every day at Midnight UTC.
